the team

Our team of passionate and dedicated professionals work tirelessly to curate and present immersive exhibitions that inspire and educate.

We believe that food is not just sustenance, but also a reflection of cultural identity and heritage, and our mission is to create a platform that honors and celebrates this connection.

At Háin Gallery, we are committed to fostering a communal and co-creative relationship with our audience, offering a unique and thought-provoking experience that stimulates the senses and nourishes the soul. We welcome you to join us in exploring the rich and fascinating world of culinary history and culture.

Háin is a creative educational project brought to you by the team | Martin toledo, Jared Maxilom, Ivan vitales, zeggy fuentes, and Kyle co.
